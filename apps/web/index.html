Milestone 1 workflow

From professional application to verified trust record.

Register

Professional creates an account and completes identity details.

Upload

Credentials are uploaded privately, checksummed, and classified.

Consent

Current consent wording is accepted and linked to timestamp/device metadata.

Pay

Payment intent or M-PESA-ready reference is recorded with idempotency.

Review

Afyalink admin reviews documents and requests replacements where needed.

Verify

Approved professionals become ready for later controlled facility access.

Security first

Designed for private documents and traceable decisions.

  • No public credential document links.
  • Every sensitive workflow action is auditable.
  • Secrets are redacted before audit metadata is stored.
  • Facilities only get controlled access after approval and subscription.
