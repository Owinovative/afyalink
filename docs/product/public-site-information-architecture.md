# Public Site Information Architecture

Afyalink's public site is organized around user intent rather than one long generic marketing page.

## Primary Routes

- `/`: executive overview of Afyalink as healthcare trust infrastructure.
- `/how-it-works`: professional intake, Afyalink verification/interview, and facility access model.
- `/professionals`: licensed professional journey.
- `/students`: student/recent graduate awaiting-license path.
- `/facilities`: approved facility marketplace and recommendation model.
- `/trust-security`: privacy, watermarking, auditability, and realistic screenshot-deterrence limits.
- `/verification`: credential review, regulatory cases, interview scoring, and publication eligibility.
- `/pricing-access`: access/subscription model without unsupported fixed pricing claims.
- `/about`: mission and healthcare trust problem.
- `/contact`: partnership, facility access, and support inquiry entry.
- `/faq`: grouped answers for professionals, students, facilities, security, verification, and access.

## Content Rules

- Real healthcare photography should anchor major pages.
- Backend-governed workflow rules must be explained without moving those rules into frontend-only claims.
- Students must never be described as licensed, verified, or facility-publishable before conversion.
- Facility marketplace access must remain described as approved and subscription-gated.
- Internal notes, raw private documents, storage keys, and audit-sensitive metadata must never be promised to facilities or public users.
